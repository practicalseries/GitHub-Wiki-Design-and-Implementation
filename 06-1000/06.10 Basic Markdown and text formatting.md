## 6.10<!--     ðŸŸ¥H2ðŸŸ¥--><img width="077" height="1" src="https://psop.uk/wi-s" alt="Spacer">Headings

Headings are created by adding hash signs `#` in front of a word or sentence. The number of hashes indicates the level of the heading: a single `#` indicates a level 1 heading, `##` a level 2 all the way down to `######` (six hashes) which is a level 6 heading.

The headings can also be constructed using the HTML tags `<h1></h1>` â€¦ `<h6></h6>`.

Headings are also covered in <a href="16.05-practicalseries-wiki-conventions#1652headings">section&nbsp;16.5.2</a>; this shows how headings are structured for the PracticalSeries Wikis and includes a lot of information about spacing and numbering and generally making the headings look better.
